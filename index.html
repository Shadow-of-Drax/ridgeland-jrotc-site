Program Highlights

Award-Winning Teams

Our drill teams have earned 40+ trophies in competitions, showcasing precision, teamwork, and dedication that sets the standard for excellence.

Leadership Development

Building tomorrow's leaders through hands-on experience, character development, and real-world leadership opportunities in our Battalion structure.

Community Service

Making a difference through service projects, from collecting thousands of food items for local banks to supporting community events year-round.

Physical Excellence

Building strength, endurance, and teamwork through our Raider Team challenges, fitness programs, and outdoor adventure activities.

Precision & Discipline

Developing focus and accuracy through our rifle team marksmanship programs and ceremonial drill competitions.

Honor & Tradition

Upholding military traditions through our Color Guard ceremonies, formal events, and dedication to service above self.
