About the Panther Battalion

Motivating young people to become better citizens through service, resilience, and excellence.

Program Overview

The Panther Battalion began as an Air Force JROTC unit and transitioned to Army JROTC during the 2025—2026 school year. We focus on leadership, citizenship, and physical fitness to prepare cadets for challenges both inside and outside the classroom.

Motto: Virtus Per Adversitatem — Strength Through Adversity

Structure: Companies Alpha, Bravo, Charlie, Delta; competitive teams include Raider, Rifle, Drill, Saber, Color Guard, and Fitness.

Army Values

Loyalty

Bear true faith and allegiance to the U.S. Constitution, the Army, your unit, and other soldiers.

Duty

Fulfill your obligations. Accept responsibility for your own actions and those entrusted to your care.

Respect

Treat people as they should be treated. Create a climate of fairness and equal opportunity.

Selfless Service

Put the welfare of the nation, the Army, and your subordinates before your own.

Honor

Live up to all the Army values. Face fear, danger, or adversity with courage.

Integrity

Do what's right, legally and morally. Choose the harder right instead of the easier wrong.

Personal Courage

Face fear, danger, or adversity with moral and physical courage to do what is right.
