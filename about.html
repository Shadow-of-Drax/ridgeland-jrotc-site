About the Panther Battalion

Motivating young people to become better citizens through service, resilience, and excellence.

Program Overview

The Panther Battalion began as an Air Force JROTC unit and transitioned to Army JROTC during the 2025–2026 school year. We focus on leadership, citizenship, and physical fitness to prepare cadets for challenges both inside and outside the classroom.

Motto: Virtus Per Adversitatem – Strength Through Adversity

Structure: Companies Alpha, Bravo, Charlie, Delta; competitive teams include Raider, Rifle, Drill, Saber, Color Guard, and Fitness.
