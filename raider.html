Raider Team

The Raider Team is the most physically demanding team in the Panther Battalion. Cadets compete in endurance and teamwork-based challenges such as rope bridge construction, 5K runs, litter carries, and more. This team embodies the highest levels of discipline and grit within JROTC.

Season

The Raider season runs from August through November with regular practices and weekend competitions across the region.

Events

To provide 6th BDE JROTC Cadets a competitive program there are 10 athletic and curriculum skilled events. The events are as follows:

    Raider Events

  • Raider Fitness Challenge
  • One-Rope Bridge
  • Cross Country Rescue
  • Team Run 3 to 6KM
  • Tire Flip
  • Obstacle Course
  • HMMWV Push
  • Raider Fitness Test
  • Relay Run
  • Raider Item Relay

    Supporting Objectives

  • Ethical Values and Good Citizenship
  • Leadership Potential
  • Effective Communication and Logical Thinking
  • Improved Physical Fitness
  • Incentive to live drug-free
  • Positive Self-Motivation and Management
The skills to work as a team member and motivation to graduate from high school and pursue a successful career.
