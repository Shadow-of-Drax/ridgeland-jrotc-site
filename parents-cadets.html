Information for Parents & Cadets

Welcome to the Panther Battalion JROTC program at Ridgeland High School. This page is designed to provide parents and cadets with an overview of our program, what we offer, and what is expected from those participating in this rewarding experience.

JROTC Mission Statement

To motivate young people to become better citizens.

What We Offer

  • Leadership training and character development opportunities
  • Team-building activities including Raiders, Drill, Color Guard, and Rifle
  • Community service and volunteer opportunities
  • Physical fitness training and competitions
  • Educational field trips and civic engagement experiences

What We Expect

  • Commitment to the JROTC core values: Loyalty, Duty, Respect, Selfless Service, Honor, Integrity, and Personal Courage
  • Active participation in class and team activities
  • Proper wear and care of the JROTC uniform
  • Respectful behavior toward peers, instructors, and the community
  • Maintaining academic and personal standards of excellence

Through partnership between parents, instructors, and cadets, we strive to create a positive and impactful environment that fosters personal growth and prepares students for future success.

Virtus Per Adversitatem – Strength Through Adversity
