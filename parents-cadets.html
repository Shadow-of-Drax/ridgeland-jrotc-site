Welcome to the Family

Welcome to the Panther Battalion JROTC program at Ridgeland High School. This page is designed to provide parents and cadets with an overview of our program, what we offer, and what is expected from those participating in this rewarding experience.

What We Offer

Leadership Development

Hands-on leadership training through real responsibilities, mentoring opportunities, and progressive skill building in military leadership principles.

Team Building Activities

Participation in competitive teams including Raiders, Drill, Color Guard, Rifle, Sabre, and Fitness teams that build camaraderie and excellence.

Community Service

Meaningful volunteer opportunities including food drives, veteran support, highway cleanup, and community event participation.

Physical Fitness

Structured fitness training, outdoor activities, obstacle courses, and competitions that build strength and endurance.

Educational Opportunities

Field trips, civic engagement experiences, military history education, and college/career preparation guidance.

Recognition Programs

Awards, promotions, scholarships, and recognition for academic achievement, leadership excellence, and community service.

JROTC Mission Statement

To motivate young people to become better citizens.

Program Expectations

Core Values Commitment

Adherence to Army values: Loyalty, Duty, Respect, Selfless Service, Honor, Integrity, and Personal Courage in all activities.

Active Participation

Regular attendance at classes, team practices, competitions, and community service events as scheduled throughout the year.

Uniform Standards

Proper wear, care, and maintenance of JROTC uniforms according to military standards and regulations.

Respectful Behavior

Professional conduct toward peers, instructors, school staff, and community members at all times.

Academic Excellence

Maintaining satisfactory academic performance and personal standards that reflect JROTC values.

Leadership Responsibility

Taking initiative, accepting leadership roles, and mentoring junior cadets as you progress through the program.

Virtus Per Adversitatem – Strength Through Adversity
