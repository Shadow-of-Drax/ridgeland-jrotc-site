Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join JROTC?

Contact your school counselor to register for JROTC as an elective course. No prior military experience required - we welcome all students!

Can I join a team?

Yes! All JROTC cadets can try out for competitive teams. Each team has specific requirements and practice schedules we'll explain.

What about uniforms?

Uniforms are provided by the program. We'll teach you proper wear, care, and maintenance as part of your JROTC education.

Does JROTC help with college?

Absolutely! JROTC provides leadership experience, scholarships opportunities, and valuable skills that colleges and employers value.

What's the time commitment?

JROTC is a regular class period. Team participation involves after-school practice and weekend competitions - all voluntary!

What about community service?

Community service is a core part of JROTC. We organize food drives, veteran support, and local community projects throughout the year.
