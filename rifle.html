Safety First - Four Fundamental Rules

1

Treat Every Rifle as Loaded

Always handle every firearm as if it were loaded, regardless of its actual condition.

2

Never Point at Anything You Don't Want to Destroy

Maintain proper muzzle control at all times during handling and transport.

3

Keep Finger Off Trigger

Finger remains outside the trigger guard until ready to fire on target.

4

Know Your Target and Beyond

Be certain of your target and what lies beyond it before firing.
