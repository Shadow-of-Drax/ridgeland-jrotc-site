Ceremonial Duties

Wedding Ceremonies

Creating elegant sabre arches for military weddings and special ceremonies, providing couples with a memorable honor guard tradition.

Graduation Honors

Honoring graduating cadets and distinguished guests with ceremonial sabre presentations and traditional military pageantry.

Award Ceremonies

Providing ceremonial honors for promotion ceremonies, retirement events, and special recognition celebrations.

Military Balls

Formal ceremonial duties at military balls and banquets, maintaining the highest standards of military protocol and tradition.

Patriotic Events

Supporting community patriotic celebrations, memorial services, and civic events with dignified ceremonial presence.

Honor Guard Service

Specialized honor guard duties for VIP visits, formal inspections, and ceremonial occasions requiring military precision.
